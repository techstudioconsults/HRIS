# User Leave — Vision

_Empower employees to manage their own leave without HR intervention, through a clear self-service experience._

## Purpose

The leave module gives employees full visibility of their leave balances and complete control over submitting, viewing, and tracking their own leave requests. It reduces HR administrative burden by enabling employees to check balance, submit requests, and follow up on their own — without needing to contact HR directly.

## Target Users

- **All active employees** who need to request time off, check remaining leave days, or review the status of a submitted request.

## Success Metrics

- Employees can submit a leave request in under 2 minutes without HR assistance.
- Leave request status (pending, approved, rejected) is always visible and up-to-date.
- Zero form submission errors due to missing or invalid data — Zod validation provides inline guidance before submission.
- Employees receive clear confirmation on successful submission via the submitted confirmation modal.

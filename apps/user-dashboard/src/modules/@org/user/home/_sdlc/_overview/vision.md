# User Home — Vision

_The employee home dashboard is the first screen an employee sees after login, providing a personalised, at-a-glance view of their HR world._

## Purpose

The home dashboard removes the need for employees to hunt through menus to find relevant information. It surfaces the most actionable items — pending leave approvals, the latest payslip availability, and onboarding progress — in a single personalised view.

## Target Users

- **Active employees** with a complete profile who need quick access to leave, payslips, and team information.
- **New employees** who have not yet completed their profile setup and need a guided onboarding checklist.

## Success Metrics

- Time-to-action reduced: employees reach the "Request Leave" or "View Payslip" flow in fewer than 2 clicks from the home page.
- Onboarding completion rate improves due to the in-dashboard checklist surfacing incomplete setup tasks.
- Zero unhandled errors on first load; graceful degradation when activity feed or quick-action data is unavailable.

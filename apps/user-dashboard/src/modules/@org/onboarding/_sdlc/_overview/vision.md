# Onboarding — Product Vision

## Problem

New company owners who have just registered have an empty HRIS. Before any employees can be managed, payroll run, or leave tracked, the company must configure its structure: company profile, teams, roles, and invite founding employees. Without guided onboarding, owners are faced with a blank dashboard and no clear path forward.

## Vision

A focused, step-by-step onboarding wizard that takes a new company from "just signed up" to "ready to run payroll" in one sitting. Each step has a clear goal, progressive disclosure, and validation that prevents partial setup. Completion unlocks the full dashboard.

## Success Metrics

- 80%+ of new company owners complete all 3 steps in the first session.
- Median time to complete onboarding: < 10 minutes.
- Zero support tickets about "how do I get started after registering."
- Onboarding completion rate tracked via `setup.takenTour` flag per employee.
